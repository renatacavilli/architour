The Born and the Ribera quarters tour

This route focuses on the medieval and specialized city of the guilds, with the austerity and splendor of Santa Maria del Mar Church at the heart of La Ribera’s Sailor Quarter, and a quintessential example of Catalan Gothic architecture. The Citadel, built under the domination of Philip V of Spain -with its archaeological remains of the Born Market, newly remodeled as a magnificent cultural center evocative of this rich history- is an important highlight of the itinerary.

The powerful Gothic-Renaissance palaces along Montcada Street and the ethereal, refined Modernism of the Palau de la Música Catalana offer a complex richness that defines this ancient city. Some vivid contemporary buildings, such as the glowing multicolored roof of Santa Caterina Market, coexist harmoniously with this medieval world.
